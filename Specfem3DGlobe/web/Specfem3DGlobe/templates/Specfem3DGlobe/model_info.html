MODEL

Istropic_PREM: Isotropic version of the spherically symmetric Preliminary Reference Earth Model (PREM) [Dziewonski and Anderson, 1981].

Transverserly_isotropic_PREM: Transversely isotropic version of PREM.

3D anisotropic: 3D mantle model S20RTS [Ritsema et al., 1999] and 3D crustal model Crust2.0 [Bassin et al., 2000]. Note that S20RTS uses transversely isotropic PREM as a background model, and that we use the PREM radial attenuation model when attenuation is incorporated.



OCEAN: Turn this option on to incorporate the effect of the oceans on seismic wave propagation [Komatitsch and Tromp, 2002b].

ELLIPTICITY: Turn this option on to make the Earth model elliptical in shape according to Clairaut's equation [Dahlen and Tromp, 1998].



TOPOGRAPHY: Turn this option on to incorporate the topography and bathymetry based upon model ETOPO5 [NOAA, 1988].



GRAVITY: Turn this option on to incorporate the self-gravitation in the Cowling approximation [Komatitsch and Tromp, 2002b, Dahlen and Tromp, 1998].

ROTATION: Turn this option on to incorporate the Coriolis effect.

ATTENUATION: Turn this option on to incorporate the attenuation.
