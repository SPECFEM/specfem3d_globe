Mars models:

- 1D KKS model:   mars_1D.dat

  A preliminary Mars model based on studies by Knapmeyer-Endrun et al. (2021), Khan et al. (2021), and Stahler et al. (2021).

  The model contains a fictitious solid inner core since SPECFEM3D_GLOBE requires this (similar to TauP).


- SH_model/
  spherical harmonics models (with a format used by Ina's evolution models)

  * case65: case study (case65_TaylorComposition)

  An isotropic 3D mantle and crustal velocity model by Ana-Catalina (Ina) Plesa:

   A.-C. Plesa, E. Bozdag, A. Rivoldini, M. Knapmeyer, S. M. McLennan, S. Padovan, N. Tosi, D. Breuer, D. Peter,
   S. Staehler, M. A. Wieczorek, M. van Driel, A. Khan, T. Spohn (2021)
   Seismic Velocity Variations in a 3D Martian Mantle: Implications for the InSight Measurements
   JGR Planets, https://agupubs.onlinelibrary.wiley.com/doi/full/10.1029/2020JE006755


   Seismic velocities are determined based on thermal evolution modeling, using a case study (Case65) together with a Taylor composition.
   Please see the publication for more details on the model.
